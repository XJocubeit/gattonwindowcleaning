The Hidden Dirt

WHAT IS TRACK, SILL & FRAME DETAILING?

Window tracks, sills and frames are where years of Queensland dust, dead insects, mould spores and moisture accumulate, forming a sticky paste that's hard to remove and can corrode aluminium over time.

Most window cleaning services don't touch them. Xavier does. He uses specialist tools, small brushes, detailing cloths, appropriate solutions, to get into every corner and groove.

The result isn't just cleaner windows, it's windows that look and operate like new, with a lifespan extended by removing the grime that silently causes damage over time.

Protect Your Windows

WHY IT MATTERS

PREVENTS CORROSION

Built-up moisture and grime corrodes aluminium frames over time. Regular cleaning extends window life and protects your investment.

IMPROVES WINDOW OPERATION

Clean tracks mean windows slide smoothly, dirt acts like sandpaper on rollers and seals, causing premature wear and stiffness.

COMPLETES THE CLEAN

Dirty black tracks next to clean glass look worse than before, this is what takes a window from 'cleaned' to 'like new'.
