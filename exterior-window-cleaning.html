About This Service

WHAT IS EXTERIOR
WINDOW CLEANING?

Queensland's climate is tough on windows. Between dust storms rolling across the Lockyer Valley, insects, bird droppings, and UV-accelerated grime build-up, exterior glass can go from clean to filthy in a matter of weeks. That build-up doesn't just look bad, it blocks natural light and degrades seals over time.

Xavier uses professional-grade squeegee technique with purpose-made window cleaning solution to remove everything from surface dust to embedded hard water stains caused by bore water and sprinkler overspray. Streak-free, crystal-clear glass every time, not just a smear around with a cloth.

Whether it's a single-storey home, a two-storey property, or a commercial building, the process is the same: methodical, thorough, and done right. Frames and sills wiped down as part of every exterior clean at no extra charge.
