COMMON QUESTIONS

Most homes take between 1 and 3 hours depending on the number of panes, their size, and how dirty they are. A small 3-bedroom home typically takes around 1–1.5 hours. Xavier will give you an estimated time when you book. We don't rush, we'd rather take the time to do it properly.

We ask that you make the windows accessible before we arrive, push furniture back from windows and tie back curtains or blinds where possible. We take great care around your belongings but prefer not to move heavy items. If you're unsure, just ask when booking and we'll advise what's needed for your specific home.

For exterior-only cleans you don't need to be home, we just need access around the property. For interior cleans, someone needs to be present to let us in and to do the final walkthrough at the end. If you have a special access arrangement, just let us know and we can work something out.
