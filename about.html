How It Started

FROM ONE WINDOW
TO THE WHOLE
LOCKYER VALLEY

I started Gatton Window Cleaning because I saw a gap, most window cleaners in the area were either unreliable, rushed the job, or just wiped the glass without touching the tracks and frames. I wanted to do it differently.

Every clean I do follows the same 4-step method: frames first, then tracks and seals, then the glass, then a final inspection. It takes longer than just wiping the panes, but the results last. That's the whole point.

I'm based in Gatton and I service the whole Lockyer Valley, from Gatton and Laidley through to Grantham, Forest Hill, Hatton Vale, Adare, Regency Downs, Woodlands, and Plainlands. No subcontractors, no teams, just me, doing the job properly.
