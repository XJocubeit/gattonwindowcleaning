01 — Logo System

YOUR LOGO VARIANTS

Four logo assets make up the GWC identity. Each has a specific role — always use the right one for the right context to keep the brand consistent and professional.

Primary Logo — Light Backgrounds
Website header, invoices, quote documents, email signatures, printed materials on white.
Primary Logo — Dark Backgrounds
Dark-mode digital, vehicle signage, merchandise, dark social media posts.
GWC Monogram — Social & Icons
Facebook profile picture, app icons, favicon, uniform embroidery, small tight spaces.
Squeegee Icon — Standalone Mark
Decorative element in social posts, backgrounds, watermarks, patterns. Never replaces the full logo.
✓ Always Do
  • Use the blue logo on white or light backgrounds
  • Use the white/reversed logo on dark or blue backgrounds
  • Keep clear space equal to the logo height on all sides
  • Use the GWC monogram for social profile pictures
  • Scale logos proportionally — never stretch or squash
  • Use the squeegee icon as a standalone design element
  • Use high-resolution files only
✗ Never Do
  • Place the blue logo on a blue background
  • Rotate, tilt or skew any logo variant
  • Add drop shadows, glows or outlines
  • Change the font within the logo
  • Recolour the logo outside of approved colours
  • Use the gold accent in the logo itself
  • Use pixelated or low-resolution versions

04 — Brand Voice & Messaging

HOW WE SOUND

GWC is friendly, local, and gets straight to the point. No corporate fluff. Just a real person doing great work in the Lockyer Valley.

😊

Friendly

Talk like a local, not a company. Use contractions. Stay warm. You're a neighbour helping people out.

🎯

Direct

Get to the point fast. Lead with the offer, the price, the result. People scroll quickly.

🏡

Local

Always mention Gatton, Lockyer Valley, or specific suburbs. It builds trust and relevance instantly.

✅

Confident

You're the best in the area — own it. Use definitive language: "streak-free", "every time", "done right".

💬

Conversational

Write like you're texting a mate. Real language. Occasional humour. Never stiff or corporate.

💰

Value-First

The $50 off first clean is your hook. Lead with it every time. Make the deal obvious and easy to act on.
