What Affects the Cost

Every home is different, which is why a quote is always the most accurate way to price. That said, here are the main things that move the number:

Number of Storeys

Single-storey homes are the simplest. Double-storey homes require additional equipment and time for upper-floor exterior windows. Three-storey or taller properties are quoted individually, just get in touch and Xavier will work it out with you.

Condition of the Glass

Windows that haven't been professionally cleaned in 12+ months, or that have hard water mineral deposits, paint overspray, or heavy algae, take longer to clean properly. Hard water stain treatment may be an additional step. Xavier will always flag this before starting, so there are no surprises on the day.

Access

Windows over flat roofs, steep balconies, or tight garden beds take more care. If access is unusual, we'll let you know upfront and confirm the price before any work begins. Get a quote →
